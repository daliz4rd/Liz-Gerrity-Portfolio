About the Client

Off the Ropes is a charitable boxing organization in Boulder, Colorado which offers youth outreach programs, and research-based boxing programming for individuals living with Parkinson's Disease.

  • The Challenge

    The client asked for assistance generating more interest in their annual Founder Fights event, the group's primary fundraising avenue. We decided on a brand-refresh, complete with a brand new website design, specifically aimed at funneling new sign-ups for the event, and launched a marketing campaign to drive donations and ticket sales.
