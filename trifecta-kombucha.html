Trifecta Kombucha

The Project

Brewing a psychedelic identity for a mystical kombucha brand in Boulder, Colorado.

Type
Brand Identity
Year
2020
Business Type
Food and Drink
About the Client

Trifecta Kombucha is an artisanal kombucha brewer in Boulder, Colorado. They use only the finest ingredients to create homemade kombucha that enhances health and tastes amazing.

  • The Challenge

    The client requested designs for logo for his new business with an illustrated style that could convey the cosmic mysticism that embodied the values and belief system of the brand. Three fairies represent the three pillars of Trifecta's essence: Mind, Body, and Soul. The pouring water represents purity, nature, and cleansing of spirit.

    The logo would be featured on all touchpoints, as well as on packaging and other merchandise.
