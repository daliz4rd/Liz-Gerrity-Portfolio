About the Client

Andriotis Law is a personal injury firm specializing in slip and fall, car, motorcycle, and semi-truck accidents. They serve victims in the Clearwater, Tarpon Springs, and Pinellas county areas.

  • The Challenge

    The client's website was failing to deliver new leads. Discovery revealed that Andriotis' brand identity did not reflect their mission them as an authoritative defender of victims' rights.
